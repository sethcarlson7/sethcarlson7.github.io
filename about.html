I'm Seth, a sophomore at Northwestern University studying Computer Science and Creative Writing.

I've lived in Chicago my whole life and attended Whitney M. Young Magnet High School. At Whitney, I was a varsity runner on the cross-country and track teams for all four years, participated in and organized events for the school's Piano program, and served as a member of the National Honor Society.

Now attending Northwestern, the unconventional pairing of CS & Writing fulfills both the logical and creative aspects of my personality. For Computer Science, I look forward to exploring more advanced concepts in software development and AI. For Creative Writing, I plan on applying to the year-long intensive Fiction track where I'll continue developing my craft by completing a novella. On campus, I've been apart of IEEE as well as RUF and Cru where I've been able to grow deeper in my faith.

Outside of my studies, I enjoy reading, teaching judo at Tohkon Judo Academy, and going on late-night walks with my dog.
